In the CSI: Crime Scene Investigation game, you become a CSI agent working alongside Grissom, Catherine and the rest of the CSI team. A classic, first-person adventure, the CSI game will test players' nerves and intellect as they question witnesses, examine crime scenes, and analyze evidence utilizing the latest in forensic equipment.

  • Join the CSI Team: Become a full-fledged Crime Scene Investigator and join forces with Grissom, Catherine, and the entire CSI cast.
  • Master the CSI Tools: Use an arsenal of forensic equipment, including fingerprint dusters, UV lights, casting kits, and much more to break down crime scenes with ultimate precision and skill.
  • Crack the Crimes: Piece together the shocking truth behind 5 baffling crimes, using forensic skills, puzzle-solving prowess, and unflinching nerves.
  • True CSI: Live the total CSI experience with real music, atmosphere, and locations from the primetime TV smash-hit CSI.
