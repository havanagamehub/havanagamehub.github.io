Milestone   Milestone

Live the experience of the current MXGP World Championship for the very first time in the entire series!

All the 2019 season's official riders, bikes and teams of MXGP and MX2 are ready to challenge you in all the official events all over the world!

BRAND NEW GAME MODES
Endless possibilities of gameplay are waiting for you! Build your own experience creating new tracks and paths from scratch and sharing them with the community with the new exciting game modes: the Track Editor and the Waypoint.

THE PROVENCE CAN BE NOT-SO-ROMANTIC
Check out what this amazing wide training area can offer you! We took inspiration from the Esterel Mountains with its characteristic rock and steep reliefs, it will be your base of operation to start your riding experience full of mud!

FEATURES:
  • THE CURRENT SEASON
    From Nequen to Shangai, with MXGP 2019 you will live all the thrills of the current championship. The competition between the riders has started but who knows who will win at the end&#133; Follow the standings and try to change the course of their destiny, even in the events where they have not raced yet! And, as in the real life, experience different weather conditions while playing. After all, after the rain there is always the sun!

  • THE TRACK EDITOR
    Unleash your creativity, bring to life the track of your dreams and share it with your friends and the motocross community! Who will realize the track with the best motocross flow? Choose between 4 different landscapes and terrains and create your track amongst the mud of the forest, the dunes of the desert, the hills of the French Riviera and the perils of the pacific-area jungle.

  • THE PLAYGROUND
    Train your riding skills and improve your abilities in the Playground settled in Provence. Roam free, race across different layouts and in different weather conditions and day times, gain unique customization rewards completing some special Trials and bring the competition to a whole new level with the dedicated game mode: The Waypoint. Create your unique path putting checkpoints on the grounds or download them online.
