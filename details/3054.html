Your life as a law enforcement officer on Europe‘s fastest roads enters the third round! Get behind the wheel of your patrol car and take on a variety of new assignments and special missions. Ensure safety on the highway and work on your career as a police officer. Patrol the dense traffic network of highways and, for the first time, country roads, and go on exciting chases. Several emergency vehicles from radio patrol cars to SUVs await you. Advance in ranks and unlock more challenging missions, new skills and new equipment and vehicles such as the civilian squad car for undercover operations. Various open world elements, enhanced interaction possibilities and an improved AI offer a lively game world as well as a diversified varied game experience. Your adventure as a highway patrolman is waiting for you! Are you ready for action?

Features:
  • Different types of police cars – including an SUV

  • Freely explorable map including both large and small cityscapes, industrial sites, major road and country roads

  • Authentic driving experience of the German autobahn

  • Accidents, attacks, traffic controls and roadblocks

  • Various mission types offering a diverse gameplay experience
