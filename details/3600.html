TransOcean - The Shipping Company is your ticket to the world of gigantic ocean liners and transnational Transportimperien. Create a mighty fleet of modern merchant ships and conquer the seven seas. Follow live their routes, gain control on arrival and departure in and out of the harbor and make for a clever loading of the cargo. Remember always: time is money. Long wait times are taboo. 55 of the largest and best-known and most exotic ports around the world are waiting to be approached by you; one of them is your home port, you choose your favorite. Each port has its advantages and disadvantages, so it is worthwhile to consider just about its location. A sophisticated bonus system provides tension in every decision.
