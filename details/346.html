The ultimate off-road experience returns with SnowRunner, a game by the team behind
MudRunner on consoles and PC. The sequel puts players back in the driver seat, daring them to take
charge of incredible all-terrain vehicles, venturing across extreme landscapes with only a map and
compass as guides!



Your driving skills are needed! Create and expand your transportation company by taking on
brand new industries and establishing your own supply networks. Provide goods and services
to your clients from the frozen depths of Siberia to the rocky heights of Michigan.



To help you in your task, drive a huge roster of enormous customizable vehicles including
some of the most iconic trucks from manufacturers like Pacific, Navistar, and more. Gear
up your rigs to overcome muddy terrain, raging rivers and other obstacles that realistically react to the
weight and movement of your vehicle, powered by the new game's advanced physics engine.
Uncover new, untamed terrains in extreme conditions, each with their own challenges, and discover the
most optimal path for your chosen vehicle to deliver your goods safely.



Complete and unlock different types of contracts, obtain additional vehicles, access new add-ons and
customization options as you progress and join up to three other players in the co-op modes to help your
fellow drivers!
