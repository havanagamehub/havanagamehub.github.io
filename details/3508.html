The ultimate off-road experience returns with SnowRunner, a game by the team behind MudRunner on consoles and PC. The sequel puts players back in the driver seat, daring them to take charge of incredible all-terrain vehicles, venturing across extreme landscapes with only a map and compass as guides!

Your driving skills are needed! Create and expand your transportation company by taking on brand new industries and establishing your own supply networks. Provide goods and services to your clients from the frozen depths of Siberia to the rocky heights of Michigan.

To help you in your task, drive a huge roster of enormous customizable vehicles including some of the most iconic trucks from manufacturers like Pacific, Navistar, and more. Gear up your rigs to overcome muddy terrain, raging rivers and other obstacles that realistically react to the weight and movement of your vehicle, powered by the new game's advanced physics engine. Uncover new, untamed terrains in extreme conditions, each with their own challenges, and discover the most optimal path for your chosen vehicle to deliver your goods safely.

Complete and unlock different types of contracts, obtain additional vehicles, access new add-ons and customization options as you progress and join up to three other players in the co-op modes to help your fellow drivers!

Main Features:
  • The ultimate off-road experience, expanded, improved, enriched.

  • Expand your transport company and drive a huge roster of enormous customizable vehicles.

  • Explore immense, untamed environments powered by the new game's advanced physics engine.

  • Complete perilous missions in more than 15 brand new sandbox maps, up to 4x as big as those of MudRunner's!

  • Overcome new extreme terrains, each with its own challenges, and find the quicker path to deliver your good safely.

  • Customize your trucks' looks and performance, complete with fully rendered interiors.

  • Play solo or in multiplayer coop up to 4!
