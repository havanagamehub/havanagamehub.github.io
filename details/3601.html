In TransOcean 2: Rivals, you will face a new challenge as the boss of a shipping company; international competition in the market has increased significantly, and the struggle to secure the most lucrative contracts has just begun. Will you manage to drive your rivals from the seven seas and lead your shipping company to fame and fortune? TransOcean 2: Rivals, the second part of the successful economic simulation series TransOcean offers three single player modes, the campaign, competition and endless mode, as well as an exciting multiplayer mode, promising days worth of gaming adventures alone or with friends.

  • In single player mode, you can master the challenging campaign or set your own pace in an open-ended game
  • Steer your ships directly in the exciting 3D mini game
  • In multiplayer mode, you can face off with other players and win victory points with the best strategy
  • Sabotage your opponents
  • Gain region points and buy different companies to rule a harbor
  • Own the majority of harbors to be the only influencer of a continent
  • Developed by Deck 13 Hamburg, the creative team behind TransOcean: The Shipping Company
  • New freight and ship types: container, tanker, and bulk ships
  • Expand your fleet and buy the biggest ships
  • Take on new, dynamic challenges and become the best shipping company
