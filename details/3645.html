World War Z is a heart-pounding four-player cooperative third-person shooter, featuring massive swarms of zombies that rush their living prey.

Featuring fast-paced gameplay and inspired by the Paramount Pictures property, World War Z explores new storylines and characters in gruesomely exciting gameplay, crafted specifically for PS4, Xbox One, and PC.

Survive the zombie swarm in four-player cooperate action with an arsenal of guns and gear.

Features:
  • Unique co-op storyline campaigns around the world in New York City, Moscow, and Jerusalem, each with their own unique environment, stories, and challenges.

  • Level up your characters and customize your weapons to survive on higher difficulties.

  • Set traps, barricades, turrets, & sentries as you gear up and strategize to survive the zombie horde.

  • Proprietary Swarm Engine&#153; by Saber Interactive allows hundreds of zombies to be rendered on the screen.

  • Zombie Swarms behave like a real crowd, getting stuck in narrow corridors or climbing on top of each other to reach players on a higher level.

  • Advanced gore and dismemberment system provides a visceral and satisfying shooter experience.

  • Procedural enemy and item spawns (which are also based on a player's performance) provide a highly replayable coop experience.

Players:
  • Single player mode
  • Up to 4 players in local or online coop multiplayer modes
