Wendros AB   Astragon

Starting with minor projects such as laying concrete for a garage with a mini excavator to major tasks such as operating a telescopic crane as you create a prestigious development. Put on your hard hat as you help a dynamic small town grow into a major city, with a wealth of regions, from agricultural to forestry, to suburbs and a sprawling city centre and port. The choices are yours. Will you create a green eco-friendly city or will you create a concrete jungle? Manage multiple projects simultaneously as you explore a freeform world, being shaped to your specifications.

As your career grows a huge fleet of construction vehicles become available to you, from trucks and fork-lifts to huge sky cranes, all realistically created in stunning 3D graphics so real you can almost smell the cement! With accurate building materials and all the latest tools at your disposal, you can sculpt the city in style! A range of missions offers a building bonanza as you create schools, office buildings and even underground car parks in your quest to be become the king of construction.

Do you have the engineering skills to rise above the bland builders to become a master of mortar with your creative constructions?
